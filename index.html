Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your Running Tribe account, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Send us an email at support@runningtribe.co.uk with the subject line "Delete My Account".
  2. Include your registered email address in the message.
  3. We will process your request and permanently delete your account data within 7 working days.

What happens when you delete your account?

  • Your profile and all personal data will be permanently removed.
  • Any remaining activity data (runs, logs) will also be deleted.
  • Some legal/transactional records may be retained as required by law.

Once deleted, your account cannot be restored. If you have any questions, contact us at support@runningtribe.co.uk.
